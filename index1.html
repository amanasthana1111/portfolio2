Jupiter voice assistant

1. Unlocking with your face: Jupiter uses facial recognition technology to securely unlock your device, hands-free.

2. Searching on Google: Jupiter can access and process information from the web, so you can ask it questions and get answers instantly.

3. Opening YouTube and playing songs: Jupiter can control your media playback, so you can enjoy your favorite music and videos without having to lift a finger.

4. Answering any question: Jupiter has access to a vast storehouse of knowledge and can answer your questions in a comprehensive and informative way, even if they are open ended, challenging, or strange.

5.Sending messages and making calls: Jupiter can send custom messages and make calls to anyone in your contact list, so you can stay connected with friends and family.

6. Activating SOS emergency mode: In an emergency, Jupiter can send your location to the police and talk to them on your behalf to get help.

7. Calling hospitals for ambulance: Jupiter can contact hospitals and request an ambulance to your location, so you can get the medical attention you need.

8. Sending emails: Jupiter can send emails on your behalf, so you can stay in touch with people without having to write them yourself.

9. Creating geofencing: Jupiter can create geofences around your home, work, or school. If you cross a geofence, Jupiter will notify your parents or police and send them your location.

10. Ordering pizza: Jupiter can order pizza from your favorite pizza place and even talk to them on the phone to make sure your order is correct.
